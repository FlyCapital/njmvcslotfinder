NJ MVC Slot Finder

We find and book New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission (MVC) slots for you

Manually refreshing the NJ MVC appointment page desperately to find a time that is close enough to what you are looking for? No problem! We can help.

You pay only after we find and book a slot you want!

How does it work?

Email the following information to njmvc.slotfinder@gmail.com

1. What type of appointment (e.g. initial permit, knowledge test, license renew)
2. Name
3. Date of birth
4. Phone number
5. Email (this is the email you get the official MVC appointment confirmation)
6. License Class (e.g. Class D)
7. Intended date (can be a range, also specify morning or afternoon if you have preference)
8. Preferred NJ MVC location

If we need information other than the ones listed above, we will contract you.

Don't worry, these information are needed to book the NJ MVC appointment (see below). We are not in the business of collecting your information.

After we get your request, our team will continuously work to find a slot for you. Once we find a slot that matches, we will book it and you will get an official appointment confirmation email from NJ MVC.

After your appointment got confirmed, you have 3 days to send us your payment. If we receive your payment in time then you are all set! If we don't get the payment after 3 days, we will cancel your appointment.
